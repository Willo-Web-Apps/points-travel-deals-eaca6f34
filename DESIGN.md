# Design DNA — Pointara

## Visual Personality: warm-luxurious
## Layout: product-led
## Typography: elegant-serif

### Color Palette
- Primary: #0F1C2E — deep midnight navy (near-black base)
- Accent: #E8A84C — warm amber/gold (buttons, highlights, glow)
- Surface: #1A2B3C — elevated navy panel (cards, nav)
- Surface Light: #243547 — lifted surface for feature panels
- Text: oklch(0.93 0.01 80) — warm off-white (not pure white)
- Text Muted: oklch(0.65 0.02 80) — soft warm gray for body

### Font Pairing
- Display: DM Serif Display — 400/italic (editorial elegance, headlines)
- Body: DM Sans — 300/400/500 (clean, modern, pairs perfectly)

### Unique Design Choices
- "Today's Deal" card as hero centerpiece: glowing amber-bordered card floats over
  a deep navy hero with destination imagery effect (CSS radial glow + grain texture)
- Typography contrast: DM Serif Display italic for hero headline creates magazine-cover
  luxury feel against the dark navy; completely unlike competitor utility UIs
- Asymmetric hero layout: headline+CTA left-aligned, deal card right — not centered;
  breaks the symmetry every competitor uses while maintaining visual weight balance
- Amber glow effect on feature cards: subtle box-shadow using amber at low opacity
  creates a "warm light source" feel — like candlelight or a lounge lamp
- Section dividers via gradient fade to navy, not hard borders — keeps the atmosphere
  cinematic and continuous rather than segmented
